‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is progressive tempo training?
Progressive tempo training automatically increases the BPM during your practice session. Start slow, and the app gradually speeds up at intervals you set. This is the most effective way to master difficult passages and build muscle memory.
Which instruments is TempoStep suitable for?
TempoStep is perfect for all musicians: guitar, piano, drums, bass, violin, vocals, and any other instrument. The chromatic tuner supports all instruments, and the metronome works for any musical practice.
How do I use the tuner?
Tap the "Tuner" tab at the bottom. Play a note on your instrument, and the app will show you the detected pitch and whether you're sharp, flat, or in tune. Make sure to allow microphone access when prompted.
Can I create custom accent patterns?
Yes! With the Premium version, you can create unlimited custom accent patterns. The free version includes standard patterns for common time signatures.
Does the app work offline?
Yes! TempoStep works 100% offline. All metronome and tuner features are available without an internet connection.
What's included in the Premium version?
Premium includes: advanced metronome patterns, custom accent configurations, extended time signatures, practice session statistics, and cloud sync across devices.
