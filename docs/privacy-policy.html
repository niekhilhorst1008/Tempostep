Privacy Policy
for TempoStep

Last Updated: February 2, 2026
TL;DR: TempoStep respects your privacy. We don't collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device. Period.

1. Introduction

TempoStep ("we", "our", or "us") is a metronome and tuner application developed by Niek Hilhorst. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application and web service.

2. Data Collection

TempoStep is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: We do not use analytics services, tracking pixels, or any behavior monitoring tools.
  • No Account Required: TempoStep works completely offline without requiring registration or login.
  • Local Storage Only: All your settings, presets, and preferences are stored exclusively on your device.

3. Data Storage

All application data is stored locally using:

  • LocalStorage: For the web/PWA version (stored in your browser)
  • Native Storage: For iOS and Android apps (stored in app sandbox)

This data includes your metronome settings, tempo presets, tuner preferences, and app theme. All this information remains on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

When you uninstall the app or clear browser data, all stored information is permanently deleted.

4. In-App Purchases

If you choose to purchase Premium features:

  • Payment Processing: Handled entirely by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android). We never see your payment information.
  • Purchase Verification: We use RevenueCat, a third-party service, to verify purchase status.
  • Anonymous ID: RevenueCat generates an anonymous user ID to validate your purchase across devices. This ID cannot be linked to your personal identity.
  • No Financial Data: We do not store credit card numbers, billing addresses, or any payment details.

RevenueCat's privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

5. App Permissions

TempoStep may request the following device permissions:

  • Microphone Access: Required only for the tuner feature to detect pitch from your instrument. Audio is processed in real-time on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.
  • Notifications (Optional): If enabled, used only for local practice reminders. No data is sent to external servers.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings.

6. Third-Party Services

TempoStep uses minimal third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For managing in-app purchases (only if you make a purchase). Stores only an anonymous user ID.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: For app distribution and payment processing. Subject to their respective privacy policies.

We do not use advertising networks, social media SDKs, or analytics platforms.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

TempoStep is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Since we don't collect, store, or transmit any personal data whatsoever, the app is safe for users of all ages, including children. Parents can confidently allow their children to use TempoStep without privacy concerns.

No parental consent is required because no personal information is collected.

8. Data Security

Because all data is stored locally on your device and we don't operate any servers or databases, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through our service.

Your device's built-in security features (encryption, passcode/biometric lock) protect your locally stored data.

9. Your Rights (GDPR & Privacy Rights)

Since we don't collect or store your personal data on any servers, you automatically have full control:

  • Right to Access: All your data is stored locally on your device. You have complete access at all times - nothing is hidden on external servers.
  • Right to Delete: Simply uninstall the app or clear browser data to remove all information. No data remains on our servers because we don't have any.
  • Right to Portability: Your data never leaves your device, so it's always under your control.
  • Right to Object: Not applicable - we don't process your personal data for marketing or profiling.
  • Right to Rectification: You can modify all app settings directly within the app.

For EU users: TempoStep is fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we don't collect personal data, there are no data protection concerns.

10. International Users

TempoStep is available worldwide. Since we don't collect or transfer any personal data, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns or GDPR compliance issues.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of TempoStep after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
